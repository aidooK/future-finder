## About This Opportunity

Türkiye Bursları (Turkish Scholarships) is the flagship scholarship programme of the Turkish government, offering full funding to international students from around the world — including Ghana and across Africa — to pursue Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD degrees at Turkish universities. It is one of the most comprehensive government scholarship packages available globally, covering not just tuition and accommodation but also a free Turkish language preparatory course before your academic programme begins.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Open to international students of any nationality outside Turkey (Ghanaian and African applicants are actively recruited)
- Age limits apply: typically under 21 for Bachelor's, under 30 for Master's, and under 35 for PhD applications
- Minimum GPA requirements apply (typically 70% for Bachelor's, 75% for Master's, 75% for PhD — confirm on the official site)
- Must not be enrolled in a Turkish university at the time of application
- Apply through the official online portal — applications are not accepted through Turkish embassies

## What You Get / Benefits

- Full tuition fee waiver for the entire programme duration
- Monthly stipend (amount varies by academic level)
- Free accommodation in government-approved student housing
- Health insurance coverage throughout your studies
- A one-year free Turkish language preparatory course before your degree programme begins
- One return international flight ticket at the start and end of your scholarship

## How to Apply

1. Visit [tbbs.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr](https://tbbs.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr) and create an account during the annual application window (typically January–February)
2. Complete the online application form with academic records, personal statements, and programme preferences
3. Select up to 12 university and programme combinations in order of preference
4. Upload all required documents including transcripts, passport, references, and any standardised test scores
5. Shortlisted applicants may be called for an interview at the Turkish Embassy in Ghana or via video call

## Tips

- You can select up to 12 programme/university combinations, ranked in order of preference — use this strategically by mixing ambitious and realistic choices across your preferred fields.
- The Turkish language course is compulsory for programmes taught in Turkish and runs for a full year before your degree — factor this into your timeline when planning.
- Türkiye Bursları is highly competitive with hundreds of thousands of applicants annually. A strong personal statement, recommendation letters from credible referees, and clear academic focus significantly improve your chances.

**⏰ Deadline: February 20 (Annual) — the application window typically opens in January. Apply early as the portal experiences high traffic close to the deadline.**
