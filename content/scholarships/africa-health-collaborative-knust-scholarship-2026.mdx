## About This Opportunity

The Africa Health Collaborative, in partnership with the **Mastercard Foundation**, invites eligible candidates in Ghana — including alumni and graduating students of Africa Health Collaborative partner universities — to apply for scholarship awards at the **School of Public Health (SPH)** and **School of Business (KSB)**, Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST).

## Eligibility Requirements

- Eligible candidates in Ghana
- Alumni or graduating students of Africa Health Collaborative partner universities

## Programmes Covered

**MSc/MPH Programmes:**
- MSc. Health Entrepreneurship (HENT)
- MPH/MSc. Global Health and Non-Communicable Diseases (NCDs)

**PhD Public Health** with research areas in:
- Epidemics or Pandemics Preparedness and Response
- Non-Communicable Diseases

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official portal: [hcmcf.knust.edu.gh/ahc](https://hcmcf.knust.edu.gh/ahc/Account/SignIn)
2. Create an account and complete the scholarship application
3. Submit before the deadline

## Tips

1. **Health Entrepreneurship (HENT) is a distinctive offering** — if you're interested in both healthcare and business, this programme bridges both worlds.
2. **PhD applicants should have a clear research focus** — align your proposal with either Epidemic Preparedness or NCDs before applying.
3. Visit KNUST's Africa Health Collaborative site for more scholarship opportunities beyond this listing.
