## 💡 Application Tips from Future Finder

1. **Start early.** Most of these scholarships require 3–6 months of preparation including reference letters, essays, and transcripts.
2. **Target multiple scholarships.** Apply to at least 5–10 at a time — competition is high but so are your chances with volume.
3. **Tailor your personal statement** to each scholarship's values and mission.
4. **Get your transcripts certified early** — this often takes longer than expected.
5. **Follow official channels** for each scholarship for updated deadlines, as they can shift year to year.
