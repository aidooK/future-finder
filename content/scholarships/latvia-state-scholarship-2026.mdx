## About This Opportunity

Latvia's State Scholarship, administered by the State Education Development Agency (VIAA), supports international students and researchers to undertake study or research stays at Latvian higher education institutions. The programme is open to applicants from countries with active bilateral agreements with Latvia and is available at Bachelor's, Master's, and doctoral levels, as well as for research visits.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Citizen of a country with a bilateral educational agreement with Latvia — confirm Ghana's eligibility through viaa.gov.lv or the Latvian Embassy
- Must have a confirmed host institution or supervisor in Latvia
- Applications submitted through the VIAA online system with supporting documentation

## What You Get / Benefits

- Monthly stipend of €130 for Bachelor's students, €200 for Master's students, and €500 for PhD students and researchers
- Coverage for stays of 1–10 months at Latvian universities or research institutions

## How to Apply

1. Visit [viaa.gov.lv](https://www.viaa.gov.lv) to confirm current programme details and eligible countries
2. Identify a Latvian host institution and confirm your placement
3. Submit your application online through VIAA before the relevant deadline (April 1 or October 1 each year)
4. Include your host institution's invitation letter and academic documents

## Tips

- Latvia is a lesser-known but genuinely quality academic destination within the EU, with strong programmes in IT, law, medicine, and social sciences.
- The stipend is modest but Latvia's cost of living is among the lowest in the EU — the two together make study here financially viable for international students.
- Two annual deadlines (April 1 and October 1) give flexibility — apply in the round that suits your programme start date.

**⏰ Deadlines: April 1 and October 1 (Annual) — confirm Ghana's current eligibility and available spots at viaa.gov.lv.**
