## About This Opportunity

Lithuania's state scholarship programme, coordinated through the Education Exchanges Support Foundation (EESF), funds international students and researchers to study or conduct research at Lithuanian higher education institutions. Lithuania is an increasingly connected EU country with a growing tech sector, affordable cost of living, and a number of English-taught programmes at the postgraduate level.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Citizen of a country with a bilateral educational agreement with Lithuania — confirm Ghana's eligibility through studyinlt.lt or the Lithuanian Embassy
- Hold a relevant Bachelor's degree (for Master's applicants) or Master's degree (for PhD applicants)
- Meet the academic and language requirements of your target Lithuanian programme

## What You Get / Benefits

- Monthly stipend to cover living expenses during your study or research stay
- Tuition fee support depending on your specific agreement
- Access to Lithuania's growing academic and tech innovation ecosystem

## How to Apply

1. Visit [studyinlt.lt](https://www.studyinlt.lt) to explore available scholarships, eligible programmes, and country-specific details
2. Identify a Lithuanian university and programme aligned with your background
3. Confirm the application process through the EESF or Lithuanian Embassy in your country
4. Submit your application with transcripts, motivation letter, and references

## Tips

- Confirm Ghana's eligibility and the specific scholarship available before investing time in this application — bilateral arrangements vary and Lithuania's list of partner countries is not identical to all European scholarship programmes.
- Lithuania's cost of living is among the lowest in the EU, making even a modest stipend go significantly further than in Germany, Switzerland, or the UK.
- Vilnius (the capital) has a fast-growing startup and fintech scene — relevant professional networking opportunities exist beyond your academic programme if you are interested in tech entrepreneurship.

**⏰ Deadline varies — visit studyinlt.lt and confirm current availability and deadlines for Ghanaian applicants.**
