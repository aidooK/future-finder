## About This Opportunity

Slovenia offers scholarship opportunities to outstanding international students to pursue Master's and PhD programmes at Slovenian universities, coordinated through the Public Scholarship, Development, Disability Care and Maintenance Fund. Slovenia is a small, scenic EU country with a growing higher education reputation, particularly in engineering, environmental sciences, computer science, and chemistry.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Open to international students from countries with bilateral agreements with Slovenia — confirm Ghana's eligibility on scholarships.si
- Hold a relevant Bachelor's or Master's degree with strong academic performance
- Applications submitted through scholarships.si or coordinated with the Slovenian Embassy

## What You Get / Benefits

- Tuition fee support for your Master's or PhD programme
- Monthly stipend to cover living costs
- Access to Slovenia's academic programmes, particularly strong in engineering, environmental sciences, and information technology

## How to Apply

1. Visit [scholarships.si](https://www.scholarships.si) to check available programmes and confirm eligibility for Ghanaian applicants
2. Contact the Slovenian Embassy (or nearest Slovenian diplomatic mission) for country-specific application guidance
3. Identify your target Slovenian university and programme
4. Prepare and submit your application with supporting academic documents

## Tips

- Slovenia is a small country with a high quality of life and low crime rate — often overlooked but genuinely worth considering for the right academic programme.
- Ljubljana (the capital) has a growing startup and innovation scene, particularly in green tech and IT, providing professional exposure beyond the classroom.
- Confirm Ghana's current eligibility status directly with the Slovenian Embassy, as smaller bilateral scholarship programmes can change from year to year.

**⏰ Deadline varies — check scholarships.si and confirm with the nearest Slovenian diplomatic mission for Ghana-specific availability.**
