## About This Opportunity

The PLO Lumumba Foundation is hosting its Pan African Virtual Quiz Competition 2026, a continent-wide knowledge competition bringing together participants from 54 African countries. Running from July through October 2026, the competition will see 108 participants compete virtually for a single trophy, testing their knowledge in a celebration of Pan-African identity, history, and ideas. The competition reflects the Foundation's mission of inspiring young Africans through education, civic awareness, and pan-African pride.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Open to participants across Africa's 54 countries
- Specific age or academic requirements should be confirmed directly with the organisers
- Strong general knowledge, particularly on Pan-African history, governance, and current affairs, is an advantage

## What You Get / Benefits

- Cash prize for top performers
- Official certificate of participation or achievement
- Souvenirs and additional recognition items
- Opportunity to be part of a 54-country Pan-African community of young thinkers
- Visibility and recognition through the PLO Lumumba Foundation's network

## How to Apply

1. Reach out to the organisers directly via email at **info@plofoundation.org** to confirm registration details
2. Alternatively, contact the Foundation via phone at **+233 20 905 7002**, **+265 884 333909**, or **+254 701 170317**
3. Confirm the specific registration process and any required documentation
4. Prepare for the competition, which runs from July to October 2026

## Tips

- Since the competition runs over several months (July–October 2026), use the early months to brush up on Pan-African history, key historical figures, and current continental affairs.
- Reach out early to confirm registration logistics — with 54 countries involved, response times from organisers may vary, so don't wait until the competition window is underway.
- This is a strong opportunity to build visibility and demonstrate civic knowledge, which can be valuable for scholarship and leadership program applications down the line.
