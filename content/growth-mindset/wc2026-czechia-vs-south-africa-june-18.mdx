## Czechia vs South Africa — Bafana Bafana's Second Test

**Kickoff:** 12:00 PM ET | **Venue:** Atlanta Stadium | **Group A**

South Africa opened their tournament with a result against co-hosts Mexico that gave the entire country belief. Now Bafana Bafana face a Czechia side known for disciplined, physical football and a knack for frustrating more fancied opponents.

This is the kind of fixture South Africa need to take points from if they want to control their own destiny heading into the final group game.

## What to Watch For

Czechia typically play a compact, well-drilled defensive shape and look to hit teams on the break or through set pieces. South Africa will need patience in possession and must avoid being lured into a direct, physical contest that favors Czechia's strengths.

A positive result here, combined with their opening performance, would put South Africa in a genuinely strong position to reach the knockout stages for the first time in tournament history.

## Studying and Competing in Central Europe

Czechia and the wider Central European region are an underexplored option for African students looking for affordable, high-quality education in Europe.

**Czech public universities** offer many programs taught in English, and tuition costs are significantly lower than in Western Europe. Several universities also run their own international scholarship schemes specifically targeting students from developing countries, including across Africa.

For students interested in **sport science and athletic performance**, Central European universities have strong sports science traditions and increasingly host international exchange students in these programs.
