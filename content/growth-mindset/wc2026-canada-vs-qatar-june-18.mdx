## Studying in Canada — What African Students Should Know

Canada has become one of the most popular study destinations for African students over the past decade, combining quality education with a clear pathway to post-study work and eventual residency.

**Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships** support doctoral students at Canadian universities and are open to international applicants, including from across Africa, covering both tuition and living expenses.

Many Canadian provinces also run their own international student scholarship programs through individual universities, and Canada's post-graduation work permit system makes it an attractive option for students thinking long-term about where to build a career after their studies.

The application process is competitive but well-documented, with most major Canadian universities having dedicated international student offices to guide applicants through the process.
