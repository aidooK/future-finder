## France vs Senegal — Group I Showdown

**Kickoff:** 3:00 PM ET | **Venue:** MetLife Stadium, New Jersey | **Group I**

This is one of the most anticipated fixtures of the group stage. France, the 2018 champions, face a Senegal side packed with Premier League and Ligue 1 talent. Many Senegalese players ply their trade in France's domestic league, which adds a personal edge to this matchup — they know these French players intimately, having shared dressing rooms and training pitches with them for years.

## What to Watch For

Senegal's strength lies in their pace and physicality in transition. If they can disrupt France's midfield rhythm early, they have the attacking talent to punish any hesitation. France, meanwhile, will lean on their depth — even without their biggest stars fully fit, the bench alone could start for most nations at this tournament.

This is a genuine 50-50 fixture. A draw would not surprise anyone, but Senegal pulling off the upset would send shockwaves through the group and put real pressure on the other two sides in Group I.

## Studying in France — What African Students Should Know

France remains one of the most popular study destinations for African students, and for good reason. Here's what to look into if studying in France interests you:

**Campus France** is the official platform for all things related to studying in France. It is the starting point for course searches, visa guidance, and application procedures for African students at every level.

**The Eiffel Excellence Scholarship Program** is one of the most respected scholarship programs in the world, funded by the French Ministry for Europe and Foreign Affairs. It supports outstanding international students pursuing Master's and PhD programs in France, covering living expenses, travel, and in some cases tuition.

**French embassies across Africa** also run their own bilateral scholarship programs specific to each country — these are often less competitive than the Eiffel program and worth checking directly with your local French embassy or cultural service.
