## Studying in Türkiye — Africa's Best-Kept Scholarship Secret

Türkiye runs one of the most generous government scholarship programs in the world for African students, and it remains significantly underutilized relative to how good it actually is.

**Türkiye Scholarships (Türkiye Bursları)** is the Turkish government's flagship international scholarship program, offering fully funded undergraduate, Master's, and PhD programs at Turkish universities to students from across Africa and the world. The scholarship covers tuition, accommodation, a monthly stipend, health insurance, and a Turkish language course in the first year.

Turkey has invested heavily in its universities over the past decade, and several Turkish institutions now rank among the top universities globally in engineering, medicine, architecture, and social sciences.

For African students who feel priced out of Europe or North America, Türkiye Scholarships offers a genuine alternative at a fraction of the cost and competition level of equivalent European programs.
