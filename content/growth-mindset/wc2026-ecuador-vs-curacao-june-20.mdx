## What to Watch For

Ecuador's physicality and organized defensive structure have made them a tough nation to break down throughout South American qualifying. Curaçao will need discipline and resilience, much like they showed throughout their remarkable qualification campaign, looking to frustrate Ecuador and search for moments on the counter.

## Studying Opportunities Beyond the Usual Destinations

While Europe and North America dominate African students' study abroad conversations, Latin America and the Caribbean offer genuine, underexplored opportunities.

**Ecuador and several South American nations** participate in bilateral education agreements with select African countries, often facilitated through embassy partnerships, though these remain less publicized and require direct embassy contact to access.

**Spanish language proficiency** opens significant doors across Latin America's public university systems, many of which offer low or no tuition fees even for international students — a genuine alternative for students willing to learn a new language for the opportunity.

For students interested in **marine biology, environmental science, or tropical agriculture**, the Caribbean and parts of South America offer specialized programs directly relevant given Africa's own coastal and agricultural development needs.
