## Studying in Spain — A Genuine Option for African Students

Spain offers African students a study destination with strong cultural and historical ties to parts of the continent, alongside genuine scholarship support.

**MAEC-AECID Scholarships**, funded by the Spanish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, support international students — including from Africa — pursuing Master's degrees and postgraduate research at Spanish universities, covering tuition and a monthly stipend.

Spain's universities also participate extensively in **Erasmus+ programs**, opening additional funded pathways for African students at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels through partnership agreements with universities across the continent.

Spanish language proficiency is generally required, though Spain's relatively lower cost of living compared to other major European study destinations makes it an attractive, more affordable option worth genuine consideration.
