## 🇬🇭🆚🇨🇴 GHANA VS COLOMBIA — ROUND OF 32

The Black Stars have made history simply by getting here — and now they face their toughest test of the tournament. Ghana take on Colombia in a **Round of 32 knockout clash at Arrowhead Stadium, Kansas City**, kicking off at **9:30 PM ET on Friday, July 3 / 2:30 AM BST on Saturday, July 4, 2026**.

This is win-or-go-home. No more group stage margin for error — one match decides who advances toward the quarterfinals.

## What's at Stake

Ghana reached the knockout stage after a tense group campaign: a battling goalless draw with England, a narrow win over Panama, and a hard-fought exit from Group L behind Croatia. Colombia, meanwhile, topped Group K with commanding form, including a big win over Uzbekistan. On paper, Colombia are favourites — but the Black Stars have shown all tournament they don't play to the script.

For Ghana, and for millions of fans across Africa, this is bigger than football. It's a chance to write a new chapter — Ghana's first World Cup knockout stage appearance since 2010, when the Black Stars famously reached the quarterfinals.

## What to Watch For

- **Ghana's defensive resilience** — they've conceded sparingly all tournament and will need that discipline against Colombia's pace on the counter
- **Set pieces could decide it** — both sides have scored from dead-ball situations this World Cup
