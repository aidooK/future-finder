## About This Opportunity

The French Embassy in Ghana is inviting qualified professionals to apply for the position of Deputy Press & Communications Officer. This is a rare opportunity to work within an international diplomatic environment, contributing to media visibility and public engagement for one of the world's leading embassies in Ghana.

The successful candidate will work closely with media professionals and stakeholders within the Ghanaian information landscape to enhance the visibility and impact of the Embassy's activities and initiatives.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Bachelor's degree in Communications, Journalism, Public Relations, International Relations, or a related field
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English (French is an advantage)
- Strong understanding of media and digital communication strategies
- Experience in media relations, content creation, or public affairs
- Ability to work in a fast-paced, multicultural environment

## What You Will Do

- Support the planning and execution of communication strategies for the Embassy
- Manage media relations and coordinate press engagements
- Create and publish digital content for public communication platforms
- Promote Embassy events, projects, and partnerships across multiple channels
- Monitor and analyze media coverage of Embassy activities

## Benefits

- Competitive salary and benefits package
- Opportunity to work within an international diplomatic environment
- Professional networking and career growth opportunities
- Exposure to global communication and media relations practices
- Direct experience in international diplomacy and public affairs

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official French Embassy Ghana application portal using the link below
2. Prepare your CV, cover letter, and all supporting documents
3. Submit your application through the official portal before the deadline
4. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted directly by the Embassy

## Tips for a Strong Application

- Tailor your cover letter to highlight specific media and communications experience in Ghana
- Include examples of content you have created or campaigns you have managed
- Demonstrate familiarity with both traditional and digital media landscapes in Ghana
- If you have any French language ability, mention it — it is a strong advantage

**⚠️ Deadline: June 2, 2026. This deadline is very close — apply immediately.**
