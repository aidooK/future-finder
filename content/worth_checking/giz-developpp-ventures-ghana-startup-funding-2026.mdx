## About This Opportunity

develoPPP Ventures, a program supported by GIZ (Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale Zusammenarbeit), is offering Ghanaian startups the chance to receive up to **€100,000 in non-dilutive funding** — meaning you keep full ownership of your company while accessing significant growth capital.

The programme is designed for Ghanaian startups that have already generated their first revenues and are ready to scale, or for innovative businesses driving sustainable change in their communities and industries. Applications opened on May 20, 2026 and close on June 30, 2026.

## Who Should Apply

This opportunity is for you if:

- You run a registered Ghanaian startup (or plan to register before receiving funding)
- Your business has already generated its first revenues
- Your startup operates in tech, agriculture, health, logistics, education, or climate innovation
- Your business contributes to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
- You have a scalable business model with measurable social or economic impact

## Eligibility Requirements

- Company is registered in Ghana or plans to register before receiving the co-financed growth investment
- Business contributes to sustainable development goals and has strong scalability potential
- Must be an innovative, impact-driven startup with proven early traction
- Open to startups across all sectors driving sustainable change

## What You Get

- **Up to €100,000 in non-dilutive funding** — you retain full equity in your company
- Access to GIZ's extensive global network and expertise
- Business development support and mentorship
- Visibility on an international platform
- Opportunity to scale beyond Ghana's borders

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official application portal: **www.developpp.de/en/application/ventures/**
2. Create an account and complete the full online application form
3. Provide your business details, revenue figures, and impact data accurately
4. Submit your application before the deadline — late applications are not accepted

## Tips for a Strong Application

- Clearly quantify your traction: how much revenue, how many customers, what growth rate
- Show your SDG alignment explicitly — which goals does your business address and how
- Demonstrate scalability — explain how the funding will specifically accelerate growth
- Be specific about your market size and competitive advantage in Ghana
- Apply as early as possible — early applications receive more thorough review

**⚠️ Deadline: June 30, 2026. Applications opened May 20 — the window is short. Apply now.**
