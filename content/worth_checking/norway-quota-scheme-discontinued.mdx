## About This Opportunity

Norway's Quota Scheme supported students from developing countries — including African nations — to pursue Master's and PhD degrees at Norwegian universities with full financial support. The scheme was discontinued in 2023 following a government policy decision.

This post is included in the worth-checking section because Norwegian universities continue to offer strong English-taught programmes with various institutional scholarships that may be available independently of the Quota Scheme.

## What to Do Now

- Visit [lanekassen.no](https://lanekassen.no/en/) to check for any new or replacement scholarship initiatives from the Norwegian government
- Explore individual Norwegian university websites (University of Oslo, NTNU, University of Bergen) for departmental scholarships
- The Swedish SISGP and Finland EDUFI Fellowship remain active alternatives in the Nordic region

**⏰ Discontinued in 2023 — no current application is possible. Monitor lanekassen.no for any future replacement schemes.**
