## About This Opportunity

Petrosol is hiring a Territory Manager for a permanent, full-time role overseeing operations across an assigned territory in Ghana. The Territory Manager will provide effective leadership for staff within the territory and hold accountability for achieving key performance indicators — including sales volume across fuels, lubes, and non-fuel revenue business, market share, receivables, regulatory compliance, and overall profitability. Candidates must be willing to relocate to any region in Ghana.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Willing to relocate to any region in Ghana as required by the role
- Strong leadership and people management capability
- Experience driving sales performance and managing key performance indicators
- Understanding of regulatory compliance within a sales or fuel retail environment
- Strong accountability for profitability and business outcomes within a defined territory

## What You Get / Benefits

- Permanent, full-time employment
- Attractive and competitive total compensation
- Leadership responsibility over a full territory's staff and performance
- Opportunity to grow within a recognised energy sector company in Ghana
