## About This Opportunity

The Business Hub is offering an exciting remote internship opportunity for a Corporate Assistant to support daily administrative and compliance-related operations. This role is ideal for students, recent graduates, or young professionals who are highly organized, research-oriented, and interested in corporate administration and business operations.

The position involves assisting with document preparation, conducting legal and corporate research, maintaining records, and supporting regulatory compliance processes — all from the comfort of your home, three days a week.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Strong research and organizational skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication abilities
- Attention to detail and ability to handle confidential information
- Ability to multitask and prioritize assignments effectively
- Basic familiarity with Office of the Registrar General procedures is an advantage
- Must have access to a stable internet connection and digital communication tools
- Open to students, recent graduates, and young professionals in Ghana

## What You Will Do

- Conduct legal and corporate research and summarize findings for the team
- Assist with data entry and record keeping
- Prepare and complete forms for corporate and regulatory compliance
- Submit and organize corporate documentation
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive company information
- Provide general administrative support to the team

## Benefits

- Monthly allowance of GHS 800 – GHS 1,000
- Flexible remote work arrangement (3 days per week)
- Practical corporate and administrative experience
- Exposure to regulatory and compliance procedures in Ghana
- Professional development and real workplace experience
- Ideal for building your CV in corporate administration
