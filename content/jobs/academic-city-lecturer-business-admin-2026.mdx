## About This Opportunity

Academic City University is a leading STEAM-focused institution in Accra, Ghana, committed to innovation, leadership, and excellence in higher education. The university is inviting applications from outstanding scholars and researchers for full-time and part-time faculty positions in its Business Administration and Entrepreneurship Department. Open positions span multiple ranks — Lecturer, Senior Lecturer, Associate Professor, and Professor — across five areas of specialisation. This is an opportunity to shape the next generation of African business leaders within one of Ghana's most forward-thinking academic environments.

## Eligibility Requirements

- PhD in Business Administration, Entrepreneurship, Finance, HRM, Organizational Psychology, or a closely related discipline (required for most positions)
- Strong research and publication record in reputable peer-reviewed journals
- Demonstrated excellence in university teaching and postgraduate supervision
- Evidence of active research engagement and collaboration potential
- Ability to attract research funding and develop impactful research programmes
- Relevant industrial or applied research experience is an advantage

**Open specialisation areas:**
- **Business & Seminars Lecturer** — Postgraduate degree (PhD not required); vast experience in programme organisation, hosting dignitaries, and coordinating high-level leadership seminars
- **General Business Lecturer** — PhD in Management Science, Business Administration, Strategic Management, or a closely related field
- **Entrepreneurship Lecturer** — PhD in Entrepreneurship or International Business
- **Finance Lecturer** — PhD in Risk Management, Quantitative Finance, Computational Finance, or Management Science
- **HRM Lecturers (2 positions)** — One with a PhD in Human Resource Management; one with a PhD in Organizational Psychology

## What You Get / Benefits

- Competitive remuneration package
- Opportunity to be part of one of Africa's most innovative universities
- Access to a collaborative, impact-driven academic environment
- Influence over programme development, curriculum design, and research direction
- Opportunity to build externally funded research projects and foster industry partnerships

## How to Apply

1. Prepare a detailed CV highlighting your academic qualifications, teaching experience, and publications
2. Write a cover letter outlining your vision for the Faculty of Business & Communication Arts
3. Gather contact details for three professional referees
4. Send all documents to **career@acity.edu.gh** with the specific **job title as the email subject line**
5. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted

## Tips

- Use the exact job title in your email subject line — with multiple positions open, this ensures your application reaches the right hiring team.
- Tailor your cover letter to the specific specialisation you are applying for. A generic letter will not stand out in a competitive academic pool.
- If you are applying for the Business & Seminars position, emphasise your network and experience coordinating executive events — a PhD is not required but relevant leadership programming experience is essential.

**⏰ Open until all positions are filled — apply early. Send your application to career@acity.edu.gh.**
