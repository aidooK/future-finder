## About This Opportunity

The University of Ghana's Research, Innovation and Development (RID) office is calling for applications from prospective National Service Personnel for the 2026/2027 academic year. This is an opportunity for recent graduates fulfilling their mandatory National Service to gain professional experience within a leading research and innovation office at Ghana's premier university.

## Eligibility Requirements

Preferred background and skills include one or more of the following:

- Accounting, Finance, Purchasing and Supply, or related disciplines
- Business Administration, Secretarial Studies, or related disciplines
- Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Technology, or related disciplines
- Graphic Designing and Infographics
- Communication, Writing, or AI-related skills

## What You Get / Benefits

- One-year National Service placement at a leading university research and innovation office
- Hands-on exposure to research administration, communication, and innovation support functions
- Professional experience that strengthens future career prospects in academia, research, or administration

## How to Apply

1. Prepare your **Curriculum Vitae** (maximum 2 pages)
2. Prepare a copy of your **academic transcript**
3. Submit both documents via email to **rid-administrator@ug.edu.gh**
4. Copy (CC) **ldawadzie@ug.edu.gh** on your submission
5. Ensure your application is submitted before the deadline: Monday, July 6, 2026 at 4:00 PM

## Tips

- This deadline is very close — submit your CV and transcript today rather than waiting until the last moment.
- Keep your CV strictly within the 2-page limit; applications that exceed this may be deprioritised in a high-volume review process.
- If your background spans more than one preferred area (e.g., business administration and communication), highlight both clearly — RID is recruiting across multiple skill areas simultaneously.

**⏰ Deadline: Monday, July 6, 2026 at 4:00 PM — this is closing very soon. Submit your CV and transcript today.**
