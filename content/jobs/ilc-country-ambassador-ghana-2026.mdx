## About This Opportunity

The International Leadership Competition (ILC) is recruiting Country Ambassadors worldwide for its 2026 program. This global opportunity is designed for passionate individuals interested in leadership, education, and youth development who want to represent Ghana on an international stage.

As a Country Ambassador, you will promote the ILC program across Ghana, encourage student and youth participation, and build a network of leaders across your community. This is a volunteer-based role with significant personal branding and career development value.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Passion for leadership, youth development, and education
- Strong communication and networking skills
- Ability to promote events and opportunities within your community
- Active presence on social media platforms
- Confidence in public speaking and community engagement
- Open to students, graduates, and young professionals across Ghana

## What You Will Do

- Promote the International Leadership Competition across Ghana
- Encourage students and young leaders to participate in ILC programs
- Organize free informational sessions about the competition in your community
- Create awareness through social media and community engagement campaigns
- Serve as the official ILC representative for Ghana

## Benefits

- Official Global Recognition Certificate from ILC
- Get featured on ILC's official platforms and social media channels
- Gain valuable leadership, networking, and personal branding experience
- Build an international network of leaders across multiple countries
- Strengthen your CV with a verified global ambassador role

## How to Apply

1. Click the application link below to access the official ILC Country Ambassador form
2. Fill in your contact details and leadership experience accurately
3. Submit the form and wait for confirmation via email from the ILC organizing committee
4. Successful applicants will be onboarded with further instructions

## Tips for a Strong Application

- Clearly describe your community involvement and leadership experience
- Mention specific networks or communities you can reach in Ghana
- Show your social media presence if relevant — follower count and engagement matter
- Apply early — ambassadors are selected on a rolling basis
