## About This Opportunity

Academic City University, a leading STEAM-focused institution in Accra, Ghana, is inviting applications from exceptional scholars and researchers for faculty positions in Materials Science and Engineering. The university is seeking candidates at the ranks of Lecturer, Senior Lecturer, Associate Professor, and Professor to support its expanding MSc and MPhil programmes. Successful candidates will contribute to teaching, research, innovation, and industry engagement in a dynamic and forward-thinking academic environment committed to shaping the future of engineering education in Ghana.

## Eligibility Requirements

- PhD in Materials Science, Engineering, or a closely related discipline
- Strong research and publication record in reputable peer-reviewed journals
- Demonstrated excellence in university teaching and postgraduate supervision
- Ability to attract research funding and develop impactful research programmes
- Evidence of active research engagement and industry collaboration potential

## What You Get / Benefits

- Competitive remuneration package
- Opportunity to be part of one of Africa's most innovative and forward-thinking universities
- Collaborative, impact-driven academic environment
- Opportunity to influence programme development, curriculum design, and research direction
- Platform to build internationally visible research and foster interdisciplinary partnerships

## How to Apply

1. Prepare a detailed CV highlighting your academic qualifications, research record, and teaching experience
2. Write a cover letter outlining your vision for the Faculty and your research agenda
3. Gather contact details for three professional referees
4. Send all documents to **career@acity.edu.gh** with **"Lecturer – Materials Science and Engineering"** as the email subject line
5. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted

## Tips

- Clearly list your peer-reviewed publications and any research grants you have secured or contributed to — this is a primary filter in academic hiring.
- Highlight any interdisciplinary work or industry partnerships in your CV. Academic City places strong emphasis on applied research and real-world impact.
- Positions are filled on a rolling basis — the sooner you apply, the better your chances of being considered in the current review cycle.

**⏰ Open until the position is filled. Apply early — send your application to career@acity.edu.gh.**
