## About This Opportunity

Nestlé, one of the world's largest food and beverage companies operating in over 180 countries, has opened applications for its 2026 Management Trainee Programme. Designed specifically for recent graduates, the programme builds the next generation of Nestlé leaders through practical experience, cross-functional collaboration, and structured development. Trainees work alongside experienced professionals, contribute to real business projects, and build a strong foundation for a long-term career within one of the world's most recognised global brands.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Must hold a Bachelor's degree or higher from a recognised tertiary institution
- Recent graduate or about to complete your degree
- Strong communication and teamwork skills with demonstrated leadership potential
- Passionate about career development and continuous learning
- Eligible to work in the country where you are applying (confirm local work eligibility requirements)
- Relevant academic background in business, marketing, supply chain, engineering, finance, or related fields is an advantage

## What You Get / Benefits

- Comprehensive, structured leadership and career development programme
- Hands-on experience working within a genuinely global organisation
- Mentorship from experienced Nestlé professionals across multiple functions
- Exposure to real projects with measurable business impact
- A clear career pathway toward future leadership roles at Nestlé
- Access to Nestlé's global network of professionals and resources

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official Nestlé Careers portal at [nestle.com/jobs](https://www.nestle.com/jobs)
2. Search for the Management Trainee Programme available in your country or region
3. Complete the online application form and upload your CV and required documents
4. Submit your application — the programme recruits on a rolling basis so apply as early as possible

## Tips

- Search by your specific country or region on the Nestlé careers portal — the programme is available in multiple markets and the role listing, requirements, and intake timelines may vary by location.
- Nestlé values candidates who can demonstrate initiative, cross-cultural collaboration, and a clear interest in the FMCG industry. Reflect these in your CV and cover letter.
- There is no single published deadline — applications are reviewed on a rolling basis and cohorts fill up. Apply immediately rather than waiting.
