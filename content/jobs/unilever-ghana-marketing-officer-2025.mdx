## About This Role

Unilever Ghana is seeking a talented and driven **Marketing Officer** to join their growing brand team in Accra. This is an excellent opportunity for recent graduates and early-career professionals looking to build a career in FMCG marketing across West Africa.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business Administration, or related field
- 0–2 years of professional experience (fresh graduates encouraged to apply)
- Strong communication and analytical skills
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite
- Ghanaian citizen or valid work permit holder

## What You Will Do

- Support brand managers in executing marketing campaigns across Ghana
- Analyze market data and consumer insights to inform strategy
- Coordinate with agency partners on creative and media deliverables
- Monitor competitor activity and market trends
- Assist in new product launches and in-store activations

## Benefits

- Competitive salary benchmarked to industry standards
- Health insurance coverage
- Annual performance bonus
- Professional development and training programs
- Opportunity for regional career growth within Unilever Africa
