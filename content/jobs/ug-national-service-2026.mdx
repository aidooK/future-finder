# Call for Applications: National Service Personnel 2026/2027

The **University of Ghana's Research, Innovation & Development (RID) Directorate** is recruiting National Service Personnel for the **2026/2027 Academic Year**.

This is a strong opportunity for fresh graduates to gain meaningful work experience at one of Africa's premier universities.

## Preferred Backgrounds & Skills

The RID Directorate is looking for applicants with backgrounds in:

- 📊 Accounting, Finance, Purchasing & Supply or related disciplines
- 🏢 Business Administration, Secretarial Studies or related disciplines
- 💻 Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Technology or related disciplines
- 🎨 Graphic Designing / Infographics
- ✍️ Communication, Writing, and AI

## Required Documents

Applicants must submit:

1. **Curriculum Vitae** (Maximum 2 pages)
2. **Copy of Academic Transcript**

## How to Apply

Send your documents via email to:

- 📧 **rid-administrator@ug.edu.gh**
- 📋 CC: **ldawadzie@ug.edu.gh**

## Deadline

> ⚠️ **Monday, July 6, 2026 at 4:00 PM** — Do not miss this!

> 💡 **Future Finder Tip:** National Service at a research directorate of UG is an excellent launchpad. You get exposure to grant management, academic research operations, and institutional networks. Tailor your CV to highlight technical or communication skills relevant to their listed disciplines.
