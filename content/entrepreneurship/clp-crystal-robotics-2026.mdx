# CLP Crystal Robotics Program 2026

The **Central Leadership Program (CLP)** in partnership with **STEMAIDE** presents the **CLP Crystal Robotics** — a transformative program designed to raise the next generation of ethical Christian leaders in technology.

If you're between 15 and 17 years old and curious about robotics, coding, and innovation, this program is built for you.

## About the Program

CLP Crystal Robotics bridges faith and innovation by training young people to build, think critically, and lead with integrity in the tech space. Participants will engage in hands-on robotics projects while developing leadership skills rooted in strong values.

> *"Raising the next generation of Christian and leaders in tech. CLP Crystal Robotics builds innovation on a foundation of faith for 15 to 17 year olds."*
