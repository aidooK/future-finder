## About This Opportunity

Botswana Tech Fund is a multi-stage venture capital fund investing in technology companies across Southern Africa. Their focus is on founders building the software application layer on Africa's growing digital infrastructure — from early-stage startups to growth-stage companies ready to scale across the region. Unlike traditional VCs, Botswana Tech Fund positions itself as a partner that brings far more than a cheque.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Tech-enabled startup operating in Southern Africa
- Focused on digitising business or building software solutions for the region
- Open to early-stage through growth-stage companies
- Founders actively looking for a value-add investor, not just capital

## What You Get / Benefits

- Multi-stage venture capital investment (early to growth stage)
- 1:1 founder coaching and regular office hours
- Peer-to-peer learning and community within the portfolio
- Mentorship and strategic guidance from experienced operators
- A partnership network comprising over $1.5 million in credits
- Regional expansion support and ecosystem connections

## How to Apply

1. Visit [botswanatech.com](https://botswanatech.com) to learn more about the fund and their portfolio focus
2. Review their investment thesis to confirm your startup is a strong fit
3. Submit your founder application directly through the website
4. If shortlisted, you will be contacted for further due diligence and conversations

## Tips

- Be specific about the Southern African problem your startup solves — regional relevance is central to their investment thesis.
- Highlight your traction, team, and vision for regional scale. They invest across stages, so even early founders with strong fundamentals should apply.
- This is a rolling fund with no fixed deadline, but do not let that create complacency — the stronger and earlier your application, the better.

**⏰ Applications are accepted on a rolling basis — there is no fixed deadline. Apply now at [botswanatech.com](https://botswanatech.com).**
