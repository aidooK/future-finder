# GSI Buildathon 2026

**Parakletus Hub Nigeria** presents the **Global South Index (GSI) Buildathon** — a hands-on innovation event themed *"Building the Future of Research."*

If you're passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems in the Global South, this is your stage.

## What is the GSI Buildathon?

The GSI Buildathon is a competitive build event where participants design and develop research-driven solutions. It's an opportunity to collaborate with other innovators, gain mentorship, and pitch ideas that could shape the future of research across the Global South.
