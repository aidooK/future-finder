## About This Opportunity

Parakletus Hub Nigeria presents the Global South Index (GSI) Buildathon — a one-day, in-person event centred on building the future of research. Taking place at the Rivers State ICT Training Centre (TechCreek) in Port Harcourt, the event brings together builders, researchers, and innovators to collaborate on tools and ideas that advance research infrastructure across the Global South. The event also offers volunteer opportunities for those who want to contribute without competing directly.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Interest in research, technology, or building practical solutions
- Able to attend in person at the Rivers State ICT Training Centre, Port Harcourt
- Specific technical prerequisites (if any) should be confirmed via the application link

## What You Get / Benefits

- Hands-on, collaborative building experience focused on research innovation
- Networking with researchers, developers, and innovators across the Global South tech ecosystem
- Exposure to Parakletus Hub Nigeria's broader community and initiatives
- Opportunity to contribute meaningfully to a Pan-African research-focused project

## How to Apply

1. To participate as a builder, scan the QR code or visit [bit.ly/GSIApply](https://bit.ly/GSIApply)
2. To volunteer instead, visit [bit.ly/GSIVolunteer](https://bit.ly/GSIVolunteer)
3. Complete the relevant application or volunteer form
4. Plan to attend in person at the Rivers State ICT Training Centre (TechCreek), Aba Road, Port Harcourt, starting at 9:00 AM on August 22, 2026

## Tips

- Decide early whether you want to apply as a builder/participant or as a volunteer — the two paths use different application links.
- Since this is an in-person event in Port Harcourt, factor in travel and accommodation plans well ahead of the August 22 date if you are coming from outside the city.
- Follow Parakletus Hub Nigeria on Instagram, LinkedIn, and X (@Parakletus_NG) for updates and reminders leading up to the event.

**⏰ Event date: August 22, 2026, 9:00 AM. Apply or register to volunteer in advance using the links above — spots may be limited.**
