## About This Opportunity

Bridge Seed Global (BSG) is a non-profit accelerator built specifically for young African entrepreneurs aged 18 to 35. Over three intensive months, selected founders receive equity-free funding, structured business training, one-on-one mentorship, and access to a global investor network — all at no cost. The program is designed to address one of Africa's most pressing challenges: youth unemployment and the shortage of early-stage capital for ambitious founders. Importantly, you do not need a registered business to apply.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Aged between 18 and 35 years old
- Building a business that operates in or targets African markets
- Open to all sectors — technology, health, agriculture, education, renewable energy, creative industries, and more
- Applications welcome from idea stage through to early revenue
- No registered business required — idea-stage founders are eligible
- Must be ready to commit fully to three months of intensive participation

## What You Get / Benefits

- **£5,000 equity-free pre-seed grant** upon successful completion of the program — no ownership stake taken
- Three months of intensive acceleration, mentorship, and structured business support
- One-on-one mentorship from experienced entrepreneurs, industry experts, and business leaders
- Training covering business model development, financial planning, market entry strategy, and investment readiness
- Direct access to investors, venture capitalists, and funding partners on Graduation Day
- Opportunity to pitch for Series A, seed funding, or strategic partnerships
- A powerful pan-African community of entrepreneurs, alumni, and partners

## How to Apply

1. Visit the application portal at [tally.so/r/D4kDbl](https://tally.so/r/D4kDbl)
2. Complete the online application form with detailed, honest responses
3. Provide information about your startup idea, the problem you are solving, and your growth plans
4. Submit your application — no supporting documents or registered business required
5. Await notification from the Bridge Seed Global selection committee

## Tips

- You do not need a finished product or registered company — BSG explicitly supports idea-stage and early-execution founders. Focus your application on the real problem you are solving and your commitment to building.
- Make sure your application clearly demonstrates impact, scalability, and sustainability — these are the three things the selection committee looks for most.
- This is a rolling program, but cohorts fill up. Apply now rather than waiting — early applicants give themselves the best shot at joining the next intake.

**⏰ Applications are open on a rolling basis — there is no fixed closing date, but cohort spots are limited. Apply as soon as possible at [bridgeseedglobal.org](https://bridgeseedglobal.org).**
