# CirculaRise Accelerator — Kenya

The **CirculaRise Accelerator** is a Kenya-based programme by the **Yunus Environment Hub** that supports **growth-stage social businesses** to develop and scale inclusive circular solutions.

If you're running a business that sits at the intersection of environmental responsibility and social impact — this accelerator was designed for you.

## What is the CirculaRise Accelerator?

Through the **CirculaRise Accelerator Framework**, the programme guides entrepreneurs in bridging the gap between:

- 💰 **Economic viability** — building businesses that are financially sustainable
- 🌍 **Environmental responsibility** — reducing waste and embracing circular models
- 🤝 **Social impact** — ensuring solutions are inclusive and community-centred

Participants are enabled to implement *validated circular solutions* — meaning the focus is on businesses with a proven concept ready to scale, not just early ideas.

## Who Should Apply?

This accelerator is for **growth-stage** entrepreneurs and social businesses in Kenya (and potentially the broader African region) who are:

- Operating in sectors such as waste management, agriculture, fashion, manufacturing, or packaging
- Already generating some revenue or traction
- Seeking structured support to scale their circular economy model
- Committed to both profitability and positive social/environmental outcomes

## What You Gain

- A structured **accelerator framework** tailored to circular business models
- Mentorship and expert guidance
- A pathway to validate and scale your inclusive solution
- Connection to a network of ecosystem players, investors, and change-makers
