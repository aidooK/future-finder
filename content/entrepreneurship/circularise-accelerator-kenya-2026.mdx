## About This Opportunity

The CirculaRise Accelerator, run by the Yunus Environment Hub, supports growth-stage social businesses in Kenya to develop and scale inclusive circular economy solutions. The programme guides entrepreneurs in bridging the gap between economic viability, environmental responsibility, and social impact — helping them implement validated circular business models. Cohort 2 is now open, supported by the Employment Promotion for Women for the Green Transformation in Africa (WE4D) programme, funded by the German government and the IKEA Foundation, and implemented by GIZ. The accelerator runs as a six-month hybrid programme combining in-person and virtual coaching, with a strong emphasis on advancing women's economic participation and leadership.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Growth-stage social business operating in Kenya with a circular economy focus or readiness to integrate circular principles
- Must assign two representatives, typically founders or CEOs, to participate
- At least one representative must be directly involved in strategic decision-making
- Particular focus on businesses advancing women's economic participation and leadership

## What You Get / Benefits

- Six-month hybrid accelerator combining in-person and virtual coaching
- Ten personalised coaching sessions — five on-site at your business premises and five virtual
- Guidance from Kenyan experts on circular operations, gender-responsive growth strategies, and investor readiness
- A graduation pitch day connecting participants with a Tier-1 Kenyan bank and other investors
- Visibility through events, international conferences, alumni networks, and global circular economy platforms

## How to Apply

1. Visit [yunusenvironmenthub.com/circularise-accelerator-in-kenya](https://yunusenvironmenthub.com/circularise-accelerator-in-kenya/) to review full programme details and eligibility
2. Confirm your business meets the growth-stage and circular economy criteria
3. Prepare information on your business model, team, and circular economy approach
4. Submit your application through the official portal before the window closes
5. Selected businesses will be onboarded shortly after the application window closes

## Tips

- Be specific about how your business already incorporates — or plans to incorporate — circular economy principles. Vague sustainability claims will not stand out in a competitive pool.
- This cohort places particular emphasis on women's economic participation. If your business is women-led or women-founded, make this clear in your application.
- Assign your most strategically involved team member as one of your two required representatives — the programme expects decision-makers at the table, not just operational staff.
