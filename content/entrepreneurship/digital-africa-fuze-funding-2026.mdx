## About This Opportunity

Digital Africa is a French-backed initiative that invests in and supports early-stage tech startups across the African continent. Their flagship investment program, **FUZE**, provides tickets of €20,000 to €100,000 to African tech startups at the pre-seed and seed stages — regardless of sector. The process is designed to be fast and transparent, moving from eligibility check through to closing and disbursement.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Your startup must use technology as a core lever of innovation
- Operations must be based on the African continent
- You must have a clear, investor-ready pitch deck ready to submit
- Open to all tech sectors — sector-agnostic

## What You Get / Benefits

- €20,000 to €100,000 in pre-seed or seed funding
- A fast, structured investment process: online eligibility check → due diligence → investment committee → closing and disbursement
- Hands-on support beyond capital — services, expert networks, and strategic connections
- Access to Digital Africa's wider ecosystem of partners and resources

## How to Apply

1. Visit [digital-africa.co](https://digital-africa.co) and review the FUZE program details and eligibility criteria
2. Complete the online eligibility check directly on the platform
3. Prepare your investor-ready pitch deck — this is a core requirement
4. Submit your application and proceed through due diligence and investment committee review
5. Successful applicants move to closing and funding disbursement

## Tips

- Spend serious time on your pitch deck before applying — Digital Africa explicitly lists it as a requirement, and it is your first impression with the investment committee.
- The focus is on founding team strength, product growth potential, and real-world impact. Make sure your application speaks clearly to all three.
- This is a rolling program with no fixed deadline, but that is not a reason to delay — the sooner you apply, the sooner you can access capital.

**⏰ Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. There is no fixed deadline — apply as soon as your pitch deck is ready.**
